ABOUT OUR PHILOSOPHY


The

Foreword is an Indian-based online book store established in 2020. Our ambition is to connect generations and cultures with a love for visual inspiration and aesthetically pleasing books. From independent publicatons to collector’s editions, we curate the finest books in fashion, food, graphic design & art, interior & architecture, lifestyle and photography.

We’re known for curating visually inspiring books, but our aim is to extend what we are really about, which is connecting different generations and cultures with a common love for aesthetically and visually pleasing books. We think that simply selling books is not that interesting anymore. People can buy any product they want online. The inspiration, the people, and the overall atmosphere is what matters most and our store is just the place where you will find just that.
